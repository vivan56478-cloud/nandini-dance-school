Classical Indian Dance

Where Movement Becomes Devotion

Classical Bharatanatyam classes for all ages — rooted in tradition, taught with heart. Join us this Saturday for your first free class.

"Every gesture tells a story. Every rhythm carries centuries."

Nandini Dance School is dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich heritage of Bharatanatyam, one of India's most classical and expressive dance forms. Our mission is to teach not just the movements, but the soul of the dance — the devotion, the storytelling, and the spiritual connection that makes this art form timeless.

We believe that dance is a universal language. Whether you're 5 or 55, whether you've never danced before or you're an experienced performer, there's a place for you in our community. We teach with patience, passion, and respect for the tradition.
